MiniBio

I'm currently a student at Eastern Washington University, studying visual communication and design. I used to be on the path to becoming an anesthesiologist, and one day I decided to forgoe the money and chase the happiness. I'm lucky enough to have a sort of natural talent for making things look nice (says family and friends), but learning through design courses has taken it to a whole new level for me, and I'm absolutely happy with the path that I'm finally walking down in life. I look forward to seeing where this career choice can go for me. I also work at Jimmy John's - they love me there, and drive for Lyft on my spare time. I love playing open-world games, as well as exploring the real world in my home state of Washington.
